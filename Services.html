Basic Plus

Our Combo detailing services brings both the interior and exterior of your vehicle back to life!

The closest to a comprehensive detail that you can get at our car washes. We blow underneath and between seat cracks with compressed air, shampoo four floor mats, clean and condition the leather seats and dashboard, wipe four door panels, for a Super Clean Interior! We also apply a coat of Simoniz Clear Coat Protectant to your vehicles finish with a safe orbital buffer. The wax is gently removed with a microfiber towel leaving the paint shiny and protected for up to 3 months. The combination of the Super Clean Interior Package and Pro Wax Package makes for an impressive cleanup for most vehicles.
